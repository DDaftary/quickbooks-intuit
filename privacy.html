Privacy Policy & Data Handling (Internal Use Only)

This Privacy Policy outlines how data is managed within our internal QuickBooks integration application.

Data Collection & Purpose: This application connects directly to our company’s QuickBooks Online account via the Intuit API. It retrieves financial, customer, and transactional data strictly for internal reporting, automated workflows, and business sync purposes.

Data Sharing: No data retrieved from QuickBooks is ever shared, sold, or distributed to third-party advertisers or external vendors. All data remains strictly confidential.

Data Security: All data transmitted between the Intuit API and our internal servers is encrypted in transit using secure HTTPS protocols.
